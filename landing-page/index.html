How It Works

From scraping to your perfect match in 4 steps

01

Intelligent Scraping

Fetches jobs from 10+ companies using Greenhouse API, JSearch API, and Playwright. Filters for FULLTIME roles from the last month only.

02

AI-Powered Scoring

8-phase algorithm analyzes title (2x weight), location, description. Scores based on seniority, domains, and your preferences. Threshold: 20%.

03

Smart Filtering

Auto-disqualifies sales, research, frontend roles. Preserves cached data when APIs fail. Collapses all companies by default.

04

Discover & Apply

Search, bookmark, and sort your matches. Click to visit company career pages. All preferences saved in localStorage.
